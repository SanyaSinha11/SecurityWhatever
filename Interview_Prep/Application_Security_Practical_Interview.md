
A strong AppSec interviewer is usually looking for:
1. **How you think while testing**
2. **How you identify attack surfaces**
3. **How you verify findings**
4. **How you assess impact**
5. **How you explain remediation**

### Q1. How do you test XSS?
1. Identify reflection  
2. Determine context  
3. Check encoding  
4. Test whether input executes  
5. Verify impact  
6. Assess whether reflected, stored, or DOM-based
